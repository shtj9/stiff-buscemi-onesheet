BIO


Stiff Buscemi is a Serbian instrumental psychedelic rock band from Novi Sad. The band draws inspiration from various types of local peppers, African spices, and Middle Eastern teas. Spontaneously formed in 2018, the band spent the next three years searching for its identity and musical expression through jam sessions, collaborations with other artists, and experimentation with sound and style. In 2022, the band began working on its first official album, “Jet-Black Highdooks”, which will define its sound and style. In 2025, the album saw the light of day in collaboration with the record label Inverted Spectrum Records.

Album "Jet-Black Highdooks"


Jet-Black Highdooks is the debut album by Stiff Buscemi that blends psychedelic rock with instrumental layers inspired by Balkan music, the Middle East, and North Africa. The album offers a rich sonic experience, highlighted by wah guitars that create an and atmospheric and hypnotic dynamic. The rhythms are intricate, and the sound shifts from energetic, unpredictable moments to introspective passages. Jet-Black Highdooks delivers a unique sonic experience that explores the boundaries between tradition and modernity through a mix of exotic tones and psychedelic effects.
