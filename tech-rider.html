2. Equipment / Backline

This section details the specific backline and equipment required for the band's performance, indicating whether it is provided by the artist or the venue.

Drums Kit (for Dušan)

Artist Provided:

  • Kick Pedal (1x)
  • Snare Drum (1x)
  • Hi-Hat Cymbals (1x pair)
  • Crash Cymbal (2x)
  • Ride Cymbal (1x)

Venue Provided:

  • Kick Drum (1x): 22" or 20" diameter preferred.
  • Drum Stool (1x)
  • Snare Stand (1x)
  • Rack Tom (1x): 12" or 10" diameter preferred.
  • Floor Tom (1x): 16" or 14" diameter preferred.
  • Hi-Hat Stand (1x)
  • Cymbal Boom Stands (3x): Required for Overhead Right, Overhead Left, and an additional spare.

Amplifiers

Artist Provided:

  • Guitar Amplifiers (2x): Krle and Banjac will provide their own amplifiers, each with a 1x12" speaker cabinet.

Venue Provided:

  • Bass Amplification (1x, for Petar): Either a suitable bass amplifier and cabinet or a high-quality DI Box for direct bass signal.
  • Guitar Stands (4x)

Microphones & DI Boxes & Other

Artist Provided:

  • The band will provide its own synthesizer (for Banjac).

Venue Provided:

Drum Microphones:
  • Kick Drum: 1x Dynamic Microphone (e.g., Shure Beta 52A, AKG D112)
  • Snare Drum: 2x Dynamic Microphones (e.g., Shure SM57) for top and bottom miking.
  • Toms: 2x Dynamic Microphones (e.g., Sennheiser e604, Shure SM57) for Rack Tom and Floor Tom.
  • Hi-Hat: 1x Small Diaphragm Condenser Microphone (e.g., Shure SM81, Rode NT5).
  • Overheads: 2x Large Diaphragm Condenser Microphones (e.g., Rode NT1, Audio-Technica AT2020) for stereo overhead miking (Left and Right).
Instrument Amplifiers Microphones:
  • Guitar Amplifiers: 2x Dynamic Microphones (e.g., Shure SM57) for Krle's and Banjac's guitar amplifiers.
DI Boxes:
  • 1x for bass if not using a bass amp
  • 1x for synth
